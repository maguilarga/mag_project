Max Temperature

This chart shows us that the temperature has a close relationship with the latitude. The highest temperatures cluster around latitude 0 (Equator) and as the latitude moves farther from 0, the temperatures start dropping. As always, there are some exceptions, but the trend is clear..

Please note that the south hemisphere is showing higher temperatures than the north. This is due to the fact that the data was gathered during January.
